# Curve AI Name And Design Options

This file is intentionally separate from the canonical investor deck.

The main deck in [investor-deck.html](./investor-deck.html) now stays anchored to the current shipped identity: **Curve AI**.

## Current recommendation

- Keep: `Curve AI`
- Reason: it is already implemented across the repo, supports a multi-surface product story, and does not trap the company inside a single wedge like call handling or dispatch.

## Name options

### Option 1: Curve AI

- Positioning: voice operations for tradies
- Best for: investor deck, platform story, expansion beyond one workflow
- Tradeoff: less literal than a trade-only name

### Option 2: Relay Trades

- Positioning: the handoff layer between customer, agent, office, and field worker
- Best for: dispatch and workflow continuity story
- Tradeoff: narrower than `Curve AI`

### Option 3: Handset

- Positioning: phone-first trade AI
- Best for: missed-call capture and voice-entry wedge
- Tradeoff: strongest on the wedge, weakest on the broader operating-system story

## Design directions

### Direction A: Current Curve AI system

- Light cloud backgrounds
- Cyan and blue accents
- Glass panels
- Serif display with sans operational text
- Best for: “AI operating system” positioning

### Direction B: Industrial field tool

- Warmer neutrals
- More copper and orange accents
- Denser field-ready controls
- Best for: more tradie-native feel

### Direction C: Premium service brand

- Softer layouts
- Higher-trust residential service tone
- More customer-facing polish
- Best for: customer confidence and larger operator accounts

## Recommendation

- For the next investor and product cycle, keep the **Curve AI** name and the current **light control-surface** design system.
- If the company later narrows harder into call handling and dispatch, revisit one of the more literal names as a campaign or product-line label rather than as the parent identity.
